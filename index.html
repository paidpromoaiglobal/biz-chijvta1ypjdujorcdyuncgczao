About Us

Thanishka Tuition Centre

Thanishka Tuition Centre is a dedicated NEET and IIT JEE coaching institute in Kovilambakkam, Chennai, committed to helping students realise their dream of studying medicine and engineering at the nation's top institutions.

Our highly experienced faculty, structured curriculum, and focused test series create the ideal environment for competitive exam success. We cover the entire NEET and IIT JEE syllabus with concept clarity, problem-solving techniques, and regular mock exams.

  • Experienced faculty specialised in NEET & IIT JEE preparation
  • Comprehensive Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Maths coaching
  • Regular mock tests and previous year question practice
  • Small batches for personalised guidance and doubt clearing
